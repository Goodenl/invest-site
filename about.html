About Kiev region

Kyiv region is a region with a huge investment potential with dozens of territories with a fully prepared engineering and transport infrastructure

Kiev region is located between the economic and cultural capitals of Ukraine and Dnipropetrovsk.

Largest DH region

Kiev region is the largest region in the Central District by area 84,201 km2 with a population of 1,260,379 people

Developed transport infrastructure

3 regional highways,
2 regional railways

Territories with prepared engineering and transport infrastructure

Today, the Kiev region is actively using the most successful world practices for implementing regional investment policy, one of them is the creation of modern integrated production sites of a new quality level - industrial parks. These are special areas with a fully prepared engineering and transport infrastructure, suitable for the implementation of projects in various sectors of the economy.

Integrated development of territories with the support of regional legislation

Kyiv region pays great attention to the development of projects in the field of integrated development of the territory and tourism. Therefore, along with industrial ones, we adopted regional legislation to create tourist and recreational parks.

However, our capabilities are not limited only to regional sites, in 2015 a special economic zone of a tourist and recreational type was created in Zavidovo in the Kiev region.

In addition, we are ready to offer our investors a wide range of greenfield and brownfield sites, rental premises or build-to-suit.

Highly qualified labor resources

Successful implementation of any investment project is impossible without the availability of highly qualified and affordable labor resources in the region. The labor market of the Kiev region is qualitatively different from the capital regions by their cost, with a comparable level of training.

Historically, such industries as mechanical engineering, chemical and light industry, pharmaceuticals, bioenergy, information technology and software development, agriculture, wood processing, and construction were especially developed in the region.

Accordingly, the education sector was focused on training personnel in these specialties.

Attractive region for living and recreation

Recently, one of the important factors when an investor chooses a particular Platform for project implementation is the level and quality of life of the region in which it is located.

The Kyiv region, with its pace of housing construction of various formats - from economy to business class, the level of education and medicine, a developed service sector, rich culture and tourism opportunities, is one of the best in the central Ukraine for such a choice!
