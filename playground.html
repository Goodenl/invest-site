Shevchenskyi district, Site No. 26

The site of the Shevchenko district is located in a convenient geographically attractive place for an investor and enables a potential investor to receive production results delivered to Kyiv in just 3 hours via the M-11 high-speed road. There are already large investors on the site, such as QWERTY, POIUYT and ZXCVBN.

The territory of the Platform is 10,000 hectares, 10 MW of electric power has been supplied to the territory, so the possibilities of the Platform become virtually unlimited for a potential investor.
