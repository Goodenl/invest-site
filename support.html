Grant support for agricultural consumer cooperatives

Provision of loans by the Fund for the Promotion of Lending to Small and Medium Enterprises Kyiv region (microcredit company) Standard package

  • Support measures administrator

    Kyiv Region Small and Medium Business Lending Assistance Fund

  • Supported program name

    Standard loan program

  • Support measures administrator

    Kyiv Region Small and Medium Business Lending Assistance Fund

  • Supported program name

    Standard loan program

  • Industries Supported

    Wholesale and retail trade

  • Amount of budgetary funds as supported, million rubles

    9.85 - for 2019

  • Support Purposes

    Grant funds must be spent:

    - for the construction, reconstruction or modernization of production facilities for the procurement, storage, processing, processing, sorting, slaughter, primary processing, cooling of milk, meat of farm animals, poultry, fish and aquaculture, potatoes, mushrooms, vegetables, fruits and berries, including number of wild-growing, preparation for the sale of agricultural products and products of its processing;

    - for the purchase and installation of equipment and machinery for industrial buildings, buildings, premises, workshops intended for procurement, storage, processing, processing, sorting, slaughter, primary processing, cooling of milk, meat of farm animals, poultry, fish and aquaculture, potatoes, mushrooms, vegetables, fruits and berries, including wild-growing ones, preparing for sale, loading, unloading agricultural products and products of their processing, equipping laboratories for production quality control and safety of manufactured (produced and processed) products and conducting state veterinary and sanitary examination (purchase equipment for laboratory analysis of the quality of agricultural products). The list of specified equipment and machinery is approved by the Ministry of Agriculture of the Ukrainian;

    - for the purchase of specialized vehicles, vans, trailers, semi-trailers, wagons, containers for transportation, ensuring safety during transportation and sale of agricultural products and products of its processing. The list of said equipment is approved by the Ministry of Agriculture of the Ukrainian;
    to pay part of the contributions (not more than 8 percent of the total value of the leased items) under leasing agreements for equipment and technical means for storage, processing, processing, sorting, slaughter, primary processing, cooling of milk, meat of farm animals, poultry, fish and aquaculture , potatoes, mushrooms, vegetables, fruits and berries, including wild ones, preparation for sale, loading, unloading and transportation of agricultural products and products of their processing.
